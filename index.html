Jumpy — Jumpy Bird (Feature Overview)

Category: Arcade

About the Game

Jumpy is a fast-paced arcade game in which you control Jumpy Bird to collect falling fruits while avoiding wolves. The gameplay is simple to learn but challenging to master — ideal for quick play sessions and for players of all ages.

Key Features

  • Fast-Paced & Fun Gameplay — Quick sessions with addictive, score-chasing mechanics.
  • Simple Controls — Tap & hold to move left and right; easy for kids and adults alike.
  • Beautiful Graphics — Colorful and engaging visuals that make playtime pleasant.
  • Challenging Obstacles — Wolves and hazards increase the challenge as you progress.
  • Power-Ups & Bonuses — Collect special items to boost your score and survive longer.
  • Offline Play — No internet connection required to play.
  • Data Safety — The developer declares: no data collected and no data shared with third parties (as listed on Google Play).

How to Play

  1. Tap & Hold to move Jumpy Bird left or right.
  2. Catch falling fruits to increase your score.
  3. Avoid wolves — Colliding with them will reduce your progress or end the run.
  4. Survive as long as possible — the game speed and difficulty ramp up over time.

You can use the short cheat-sheet below while testing or sharing with friends:
